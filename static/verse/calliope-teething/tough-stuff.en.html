Calliope Teething

Tough Stuff

What, then, is the reason for so much affliction?
That our minds are always in motion?
Difficulties one finds in communication?
No, for nevertheless we air
The maddening enclosure,
And in containment
Find relief:
At every trap of torment,
Perfect composure in perfect grief.
