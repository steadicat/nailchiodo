Calliope Teething

For J.M.

Gratitude beyond imagination
Like an immaculate panther hides
Beside half-forgotten trees,
Growling out of range of gun-fire,
Growling for your last breath,
Awaits to leap once and devour.

  1. all known panthers, including the black type, are in reality spotted and members of the genus Pantera maculata. ↩
